About Huntington Beach Bicycles

Since 1965, we've been serving the Orange County cycling community from our location at 15862 Springdale Street. For the past 30 years, we've provided outstanding value and extensive selections of quality bikes and related items, along with trusted service to local and surrounding areas.

We're committed to helping every customer realize their cycling vision, whether it's cruising the boardwalk or hitting the trails.

Virtuous and/or Value driven, Huntington Beach Bicycles is committed to helping with everyone's cycling needs and tries hard to help each customer realize their cycling vision!

H.B. Bikes is here to assist with all aspects of service, appreciates everybody's business and likes to hear from customers; so, if anything comes to mind (questions/comments) while "cruising" through this site, direct e-mails to: hbbikes@hbbikes.com
